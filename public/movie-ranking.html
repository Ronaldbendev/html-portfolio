The Best Movies According to Ronald Ben

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Secondhand Lion

This is one of my favorite movies. It feels authentic, with a compelling story and an unexpected twist at the end.

Game of Thrones

One of my favorite series because of its numerous plot twists and outstanding casting. Each episode offers valuable lessons to learn.

Attack on Titan

I enjoyed predicting most of the Titans and had a theory that all Titans originated from humans, which added to my excitement while watching.
